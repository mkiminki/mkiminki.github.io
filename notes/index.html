Resources & Notes

Here are some notes on astronomy-related software and data reduction that I've put together over the years.

Most of these were last updated in 2017. I've tried to remove anything that's become obviously obsolete,‡ but I make no guarantees.

  • Tips for using IRAF's apall task for aperture extraction.
  • HeNeAr and HgNeAr spectral atlases for wavelength calibration on the MMT Observatory's multi-fiber Hectospec spectrograph.
  • My sky subtraction program “Hectosky,” designed for fiber spectra in regions with complicated background emission.

‡For instance, if you're looking for how to install IRAF, you'll need to check out the IRAF Community Distribution.
