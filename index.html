Welcome! I'm Megan Kiminki, astrophysicist and coder. I received my PhD from the University of Arizona, where I studied how massive stars affect their surroundings. My work includes statistical analysis, processing of spectral and imaging data, and the development of user-friendly tools for handling large data sets.
