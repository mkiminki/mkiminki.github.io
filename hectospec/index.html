Hectospec Wavelength Calibration

I made a set of reference comparison spectra for use with the MMT Observatory's Hectospec spectrograph. In my experience, Hectospec's helium-neon-argon (HeNeAr) lamps produce spectra that do not look like the the typical HeNeAr atlas spectra, largely because the helium lamps are stronger or weak. In addition, medium-resolution Hectospec spectra often use the PenRay mercury-neon-argon (HgNeAr) lamps, on their own or with the helium lamps also turned on, and HgNeAr spectral atlases were practically non-existent.
